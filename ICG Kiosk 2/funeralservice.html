Notify Imam
Contact the Imam or religious leader to inform about the loss and arrange for the funeral service.
Prepare Janazah
Prepare the deceased body for the Janazah (funeral) prayer according to Islamic rites.
Wash Body
Perform the ritual washing (Ghusl) of the deceased's body in accordance with Islamic tradition.
Shroud Body
Wrap the washed body in a simple shroud (Kafan) as per Islamic guidelines.
Perform Janazah Prayer
Gather for the Janazah prayer, which is a collective obligation upon the Muslim community, and offer supplications for the deceased.
Transport to Cemetery // Location: 4925 Harvest Rd, McLeansville, NC 27301
Transport the deceased to the cemetery for burial, accompanied by family and community members.
